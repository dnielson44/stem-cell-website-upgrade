What We Do

As orthopedic surgeons and musculoskeletal physicians we have noticed much interest in the community regarding regenerative medicine and the use of stem cells. We have also noticed that there is a wide spectrum of treatment possibilities and no regulation or oversight as to how these treatments are performed or the quality of the actual product the patients is receiving. As comprehensive providers of musculoskeletal medicine, we feel that is in our patients best interest for us to provide responsible, competent and cutting edge medical care. You should have a consultation with one of our providers before spending large amounts of money on regenerative medicine in any other venue

Our Goal

Here at Coral Desert Orthopedics we are prepared to use the latest innovations in science and technology to help promote a successful surgical outcome and to maintain a healthy, active lifestyle.
